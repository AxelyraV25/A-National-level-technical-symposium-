Technical Events

Paper X

Present Your Innovation!!!

  • Impress expert judges with your technical depth and presentation skills.
  • Paper must be original. Related domains: AI & ML, Cybersecurity & Blockchain, Green Computing & Sustainability, Human-Computer Interaction, Robotics & Automation, Recent Trends in Technology.
  • Team Size: 1–3 members
  • Time Limit: 4 mins presentation + 2 mins Q&A
  • Medium: PowerPoint (Max 10 slides)
  • Submission: Send your paper (IEEE format) & PPT to axelyrav.25@gmail.com
  • Judging Criteria: Innovation | Technical Depth | Clarity | Presentation | Q&A Skills

Prompt Craft

On-spot AI Image Generation Challenge. Craft prompts and generate the best AI images!!!

  • Theme: Provided on the spot — use AI tools to generate the most creative and accurate image. Your prompt skills will guide the AI’s imagination!
  • Team Size: Individual Entry
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Tools Allowed: ChatGPT (DALL·E), Bing Image Creator, or any free AI image generator
  • Judging Criteria: Prompt Creativity | Relevance to Theme | Visual Appeal | Originality

Error 404

Debugging Challenge. Spot and fix errors in real code!!!

  • No need to write from scratch, just debug like a pro.
  • Team Size: Individual Entry
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Format: 20 questions
  • Language: Python
  • Judging Criteria: Syntax Correctness | Code Logic | Time Taken | No External Tools Allowed

AIQ

AI Knowledge Quiz with 20 MCQs to test your AI understanding and trends!!!

  • All answers on OMR sheets
  • Team Size: Individual Entry
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Format: 20 Questions (10 Easy | 5 Medium | 5 Hard)
  • Topics: AI Fundamentals | ML Basics | Data & Algorithms | AI Applications | Tech Trends
  • Judging Criteria: +1 point per correct answer | No negative marking | Top scorers will be awarded

Non-Technical Events

Try Not to Laugh

  • Description: THE ULTIMATE REACTION GAME. Watch hilarious videos, memes, and comic acts — but no smiling, no laughing, not even a smirk. Outlast everyone and be the last stone-faced champion!
  • Entry: Individual
  • Duration: 20 mins
  • Rules: Monitored throughout. If you laugh, smile, or smirk, YOU'RE OUT!
  • Judging Criteria: Expressions monitored by coordinators. Last one without reacting wins.

Guess in Mute

  • Description: THE SILENT MADNESS GAME. No voice, no sound — just expressions and lip-reading. Teams rely purely on non-verbal communication!
  • Team Size: 2 members
  • Duration: 20 mins
  • Rounds:
    • Round 1: One teammate wears headphones, the other mouths phrases.
    • Round 2: Teams guess dialogue or movie name from muted movie clip.
  • Judging Criteria: +1 point per correct guess. Highest scoring team wins.

AdMad

  • Description: GUESS THE AD BEAT! Hear iconic advertisement jingles, background music, or voices from famous ads and guess the brand/product.
  • Team Size: 1–2 members
  • Duration: 20 mins
  • Rules: Raise hand to answer. Multiple rounds from easy to hard.
  • Judging Criteria: +10 points per correct answer. No negative marking. Highest scoring team wins.

About Axelyra V.25

Axelyra is more than just a symposium, it’s a fusion of Axe, symbolizing precision and strength, and Lyra, the constellation that inspires limitless exploration. Representing the sharpness of intellect and the brilliance of innovation, Axelyra V.25 stands as a beacon for young minds ready to carve their mark in technology and beyond. Here, creativity meets competition, and ideas transform into impact.

This is your stage. Whether you’re here to present your groundbreaking research, test your skills in high-energy challenges, or simply absorb the sparks of inspiration, Axelyra is where you belong. Step in with your ideas, your curiosity, and your ambition and leave with experiences, connections, and victories that will shape your journey.
