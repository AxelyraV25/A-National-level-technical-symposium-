Technical Events

  • Present Your Innovation to the World! Showcase your original research or tech idea in the fields of AI, Data Science, Computer Science, or other emerging technologies. Impress expert judges with your technical depth and presentation skills.
  • Paper must be original and related domains are AI&ML,Cybersecurity & blockchain,Green Computing & Sustainability,Human-Computer Interaction,Robotics & Automation,Recent trends in Technology.
  • Team Size: 1–3 members.
  • Time Limit: 4 mins presentation + 2 mins Q&A
  • Medium: PowerPoint (Max 10 slides)
    Submission: Send your paper (IEEE format) & PPT to axelyrav.25@gmail.com
    Judging Criteria: Innovation | Technical Depth | Clarity | Presentation | Q&A Skills .
  • ON-SPOT AI IMAGE GENERATION BATTLE Unleash Creativity with AI! Get a theme on the spot. Craft the perfect prompt. Use AI tools to generate the most creative and accurate image. Your prompt skills will guide the AI’s imagination!
  • Team Size: Individual Entry
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Tools Allowed: ChatGPT (DALL·E), Bing Image Creator, or any free AI image generator
  • Judging Criteria: Prompt Creativity | Relevance to Theme | Visual Appeal | Originality
  • DEBUGGING CHALLENGE Fix the Bug. Beat the Clock. Choose a language: C, Java, or Python. Spot and correct syntax & logic errors in real code. No need to write from scratch, just debug like a pro.
  • Team Size: 1–2 members
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Format: 1 Easy, 1 Medium, 1 Hard question
  • Languages: C, Java, or Python (choose any one)
  • Judging Criteria: Syntax Correctness | Code Logic | Time Taken | No External Tools Allowed
  • THE ULTIMATE AI KNOWLEDGE QUIZ How well do you know AI and its future? Tackle 45 multiple-choice questions designed to test your knowledge of Artificial Intelligence concepts, trends, and future technologies. All answered on OMR sheets
  • Team Size: 1-2 members
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Format: 20 Questions (10 Easy | 5 Medium | 5 Hard)
  • Topics Include: AI Fundamentals | ML Basics | Data & Algorithms | AI Applications | Tech Trends
  • Judging Criteria: +1 points per correct answer | No negative marking | Top scorers will be awarded

Non-Technical Events

  • THE ULTIMATE REACTION GAME Can you keep a straight face? Watch hilarious videos, memes, and comic acts, but here's the twist: no smiling, no laughing, not even a smirk. Outlast everyone and be the last stone-faced champion!
  • Individual Entry
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Rules: You'll be continuously monitored throughout | If you laugh, smile, or even smirk, YOU'RE OUT!
  • Judging Criteria: Expressions monitored by coordinators | Last one without reacting wins
  • THE SILENT MADNESS GAME No voice. No sound. Just expressions and lip-reading. A chaotic and hilarious game where teammates rely purely on non-verbal communication!
  • Team Size: 2 members
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Round 1: One teammate wears headphones with loud music. The other mouths fun phrases.
  • Round 2: Teams watch a muted movie clip. They must guess either the dialogue or movie name
  • Judging Criteria: 2 minutes to guess as many as possible |+1 points per correct guess | Highest scoring team wins
  • GUESS THE AD BEAT! Time to put your memory to the test! You’ll hear iconic advertisement jingles, background music, or voices from famous ads and you have to guess the brand or product.
  • Team Size: 1–2 members
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Audio Types: Advertising Jingles | Voiceovers | Background Brand Sounds
  • Rules: Raise your hand and answer | Multiple rounds – Easy to Hard
  • Judging Criteria: +10 points per correct answer | No negative marking | Highest scoring team wins
